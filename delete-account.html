Delete Account – BTC Miner Simulator

This page explains how users of BTC Miner Simulator can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the BTC Miner Simulator app.

  1. Open the BTC Miner Simulator app.
  2. Go to the Settings section.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

Once confirmed, your account deletion request will be processed.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (email and user ID)
  • Your mining progress and virtual rewards
  • Your referral data
  • App activity linked to your account

Data Retention

Some information may be temporarily retained for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes.

Such data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
